Demonstration Control Emulator

ICAM Technologies Corporation offers Control Emulator, an ISO-based simulation product allowing NC users to simulate and test NC programs from ISO machine codes within CATIA / DELMIA V5. Control Emulator provides a new and powerfull way to validate NC programs and allows for specific modifications based on simulation results.

This integrated solution enables users to easily locate G and M codes and determine their association to specific operations within the NC program.

ICAM invites you to try the following demonstrations. The demos contain fully functional Control Emulators with sample parts and machine models. Do not hesitate to contact an ICAM representative for more details or questions you may have regarding our products and services.
